Affordances & Signifiers I

Design, price, and reliability are to products as protein, fruit, and vegetables are to the human body. Affordances are what we can do with things, and signifiers hint at how we use things. This chapter clearly defines an explains these key concepts. Beyond definitions, this section hones in on not only what makes a bad design, but how important a good design is as well. However, we don't lose sight of the bigger picture - the idea that a balance of considerations is key. The only metric that really matters is if people enjoy using it.
