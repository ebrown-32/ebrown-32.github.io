Design Systems


Today we will be looking at https://www.designsystems.com/how-a-small-team-built-figma-dot-coms-design-system/. There are some very simple lessons to be learned from this article.

"Simplify"

As we start our final projects, I can't help but recognize how the instructions put an emphasis on identifying and developing reusable components. At first I thought this might be overthinking it a little bit, but the scale of my final project is relatively small. As soon as you start designing things at scale, a consistent look is nearly impossible without reusing proven, consistent components.

So what?

Sure we define some nice definitions in this chapter. But to build a good product, you have to knock everything out of the park. Design included.
