Navigation & Values





Understanding our core values: An exercise for individuals and teams. (2020, February 2). Colin Breck. https://blog.colinbreck.com/understanding-our-core-values-an-exercise-for-individuals-and-teams/

"Collaborative"

The whole process of identifying your values in the context of a team is collaborative. Identifying these values for yourself is one thing, but it doesn't mean much if you aren't able to open up to your teammates and share with them. Listening and absorbing values that your teammates have identified is just as crucial.

So what?

I really like the idea of re-examining values over time. Especially as a relatively young person, I seem to be constantly changing and growing. A great takeaway from this article is how important it is to identify where your priorities lie, so that you can better figure out ways to help your team.
