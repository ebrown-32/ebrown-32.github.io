Design System Workshop


Today we will be looking at https://www.designsystems.com/how-a-small-team-built-figma-dot-coms-design-system/. There are some very good lessons to be learned from this article.

"Simplify"

As we start our final projects, I can't help but recognize how the instructions put an emphasis on identifying and developing reusable components. At first I thought this might be overthinking it a little bit, but the scale of my final project is relatively small. As soon as you start designing things at scale, a consistent look is nearly impossible without reusing proven, consistent components.

So what?

Taking time to group elements and name things will actually save a bunch of time in the long run. Doing it right the first time will help you do it right the second and third time, as opposed to having to redo it all at the end.
